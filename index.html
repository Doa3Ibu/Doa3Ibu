Taste the trend,

The Daapoer

The Daapooer is a trendy food spot known for serving up delicious Japanese-inspired dishes with a modern twist. The menu features favorites like crispy Karaage, savory Katsu Burgers, light and crunchy Tempura, and hearty Japanese Curry. Each dish is crafted with care, blending authentic flavors with a contemporary touch to deliver a memorable dining experience. Whether you're craving a quick bite or a full meal, The Daapooer promises bold flavors and satisfying portions. With the slogan "Taste the Trend, Bites through The Daapooer," this spot is all about bringing fresh, popular tastes to the table while keeping it exciting for every foodie.

Our best Seller Menu

Katsu Burger
IDR 18k

A katsu burger features a crispy, breaded chicken or beef cutlet inside soft buns, topped with fresh lettuce and drizzled with tangy tonkatsu sauce. The perfect mix of crunch, juiciness, and savory flavor in every bite.

Original Udon
IDR 22k

Original udon features thick, chewy noodles in a light, savory broth made from soy sauce and dashi, garnished with green onions or tempura flakes for added flavor and texture.

Chicken Kaarage
IDR 18k

Chicken karaage is a Japanese dish of bite-sized chicken pieces marinated in soy sauce, ginger, and garlic, then lightly coated in flour and fried until golden and crispy. It's juicy on the inside, with a perfect crunch on the outside.

Shrimp tempura
IDR 15k

Shrimp tempura features large, tender shrimp coated in a light, airy batter and deep-fried until crisp and golden. The delicate crunch pairs perfectly with a dipping sauce, offering a savory and satisfying bite.

With that idea in mind, The Daapooer was born.
